EasyQR

With EasyQR, you can easily create customized QR codes!

Features

  • QR code color selection
  • QR code elements size selection
  • QR code saving formats selection
  • Reading QR codes from images with any colors
  • Scanning QR codes from camera
  • Saving QR codes in PNG, JPG, and SVG formats
  • Theme selection light or dark
  • App font selection

Planned Features

  • Analytics for QR code scans
  • Logo adding in QR codes
  • Customizable QR code shapes
